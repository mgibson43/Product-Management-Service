Our Founding

Piano Parlor began with the simple yet powerful realization that struck Mr. Gamgee. "I like pianos," thought Mr. Gamgee. From that moment, Mr. Gamgee knew what he had to do, he had to create a piano store with selling power so potent that it could rival the one ring. And thus, Piano Parlor was born.

What We Do

Here at Piano Parlor, we pride ourselves on the variety and quality of our pianos and piano accessories. We will stop at nothing to make sure you get your perfect piano, and we have created the perfect store to help us help you find it. Our store page has the options you're looking for and more! If the product you're looking for isn't on our page, have no fear, you can add it to our store yourself!!! That is the magic of Piano Parlor.
